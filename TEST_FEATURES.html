Feature 1: Edit Profile

Edit and customize your user profile information with a beautiful modal interface.

1 Open the Dashboard (index.html)
2 Click the "Edit Profile" button on your profile card
3 Fill in your details (Name, Email, Title, Bio)
4 Click "Save Changes"
5 Watch your profile update automatically!

What Can You Edit?

  • ✓ Full Name
  • ✓ Email Address (with validation)
  • ✓ Job Title
  • ✓ Professional Bio

Feature 2: Download Report

Generate and download a professionally formatted PDF report of your resume analysis.

1 Navigate to the Analysis Results page
2 Look for "Download Report" button in the header
3 Click the button
4 Print dialog opens with your formatted report
5 Choose "Save as PDF" or Print

Report Includes:

  • ✓ Overall Score (85/100)
  • ✓ Analysis Details (file, role, date)
  • ✓ Performance Metrics Breakdown
  • ✓ Skill Analysis (matched & missing)
  • ✓ Improvement Suggestions
  • ✓ Professional Summary
